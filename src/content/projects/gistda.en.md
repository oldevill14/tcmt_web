## Project Overview

The Geo-Informatics and Space Technology Development Agency (GISTDA) is a high-technology government agency that requires reliable, high-performance IT infrastructure to handle large volumes of geospatial and satellite data. TCM Technology Co., Ltd. was engaged to design and upgrade the network and server infrastructure to meet these demanding requirements.

## Scope of Work

- Design of a high-performance network architecture
- Installation of new Core and Distribution Layer network equipment
- Structured cabling throughout the building
- Procurement and installation of servers and high-capacity storage
- Firewall and access control configuration
- Performance and stability testing prior to handover
- Preparation of network diagrams and operational documentation

## Outcomes

The upgraded IT infrastructure handles large geospatial and satellite data workloads with high availability, ensuring GISTDA can carry out its space technology mission without interruption.
