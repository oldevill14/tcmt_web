## Project Overview

The Department of Employment, Ministry of Labour, engaged TCM Technology Co., Ltd. to design, procure, and install IT infrastructure for its central office, in order to support employment services and an increasingly digital-first service delivery model.

## Scope of Work

- Design of network and server architecture for the central office
- Installation and configuration of servers and storage with virtualisation
- LAN installation throughout the building to structured cabling standards
- Deployment of Active Directory and Identity & Access Management (IAM)
- Implementation of a basic Backup and Disaster Recovery solution
- System testing, handover, and full documentation
- After-sales support and ongoing maintenance

## Outcomes

The upgraded IT environment supports concurrent work by a large number of staff, accommodates growth in online service users, and reduces processing time for public service requests.
