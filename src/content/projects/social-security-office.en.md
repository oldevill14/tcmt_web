## Project Overview

The Social Security Office (SSO) manages large volumes of subscriber data and places the highest priority on information security. TCM Technology Co., Ltd. was engaged to upgrade its network infrastructure and strengthen IT security posture in alignment with government policies and best practices.

## Scope of Work

- Risk and vulnerability assessment of the existing network
- Upgrade to next-generation Firewall and IPS/IDS
- Improved network segmentation to isolate sensitive data zones
- VPN deployment for secure remote office connectivity
- Basic Log Management and SIEM configuration
- Post-upgrade security assessment
- Development of internal IT security policies and user guides

## Outcomes

The upgraded security environment strengthens protection of subscriber data. Network segmentation reduces the blast radius of any potential attack, and the SIEM enables the IT team to detect suspicious events more rapidly.
