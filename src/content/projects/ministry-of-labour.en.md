## Project Overview

The Ministry of Labour engaged TCM Technology Co., Ltd. to design and upgrade its IT infrastructure to support labour services nationwide. The scope covered internal LAN, inter-agency WAN connectivity, and a centralised network management and monitoring system.

## Scope of Work

- Site survey and assessment of existing infrastructure; preparation of upgrade roadmap
- Design and installation of high-performance LAN throughout the ministry's office building
- Structured cabling installation to TIA/EIA standards, including patch panels and cable management
- Deployment of Access Switches, Core Switches, and enterprise-grade Firewall
- WAN connectivity linking the central ministry to affiliated regional offices
- Configuration of a centralised Network Management System (NMS)
- Staff training for system administrators and preparation of operational manuals

## Outcomes

The new network infrastructure handles significantly higher data volumes required by the ministry's expanding digital services. Built-in redundancy ensures continuity of public-facing services and reduces the risk of outages. Administrative staff can monitor network health and resolve issues through a centralised dashboard.
