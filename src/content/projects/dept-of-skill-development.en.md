## Project Overview

The Department of Skill Development, Ministry of Labour, engaged TCM Technology Co., Ltd. to design and install IT infrastructure — including a mini data centre — to support the expansion of its information systems and nationwide vocational training services.

## Scope of Work

- Design and construction of a server room compliant with relevant standards
- Installation of UPS (uninterruptible power supply) and precision air-conditioning for the server room
- Rack installation and cable management within the server room
- Deployment of servers, storage, and core networking equipment
- LAN cabling and structured cabling throughout the building
- Basic network security configuration
- Training for system administrator staff

## Outcomes

The newly built, standards-compliant server room accommodates the department's growing information systems. UPS and precision cooling keep equipment running safely and continuously, reducing the risk of downtime from power outages or overheating.
