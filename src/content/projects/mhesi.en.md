## Project Overview

The Ministry of Higher Education, Science, Research and Innovation (MHESI) required modern IT infrastructure to support coordination between higher education institutions and research agencies nationwide. TCM Technology Co., Ltd. was engaged to deploy network infrastructure and server systems across the ministry's office premises.

## Scope of Work

- Infrastructure requirements survey and analysis
- Network architecture design for the ministry's office building
- Installation of Core, Distribution, and Access Switches
- Structured cabling installation across all floors
- Server deployment and configuration for internal systems
- Wireless LAN installation covering meeting rooms and workspaces
- System testing, handover, and full documentation

## Outcomes

The new network infrastructure supports efficient communication and data exchange among ministry staff and affiliated agencies. It accommodates online meetings and digital document management workflows, enhancing productivity for a modern, digital-first ministry.
