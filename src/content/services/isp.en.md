## High-speed Internet Services for Organizations

TCM Technology Co., Ltd. delivers high-speed internet connectivity for organizations and agencies of every size. Our extensive, high-stability network, backed by a responsive support team, keeps your business running without interruption.

### Scope of work

- Dedicated and shared internet services for organizations
- Speeds ranging from 10 Mbps to 10 Gbps to match your requirements
- Redundancy design for continuous, high-availability connectivity
- Automatic load balancing and failover
- 24/7 traffic monitoring and oversight
- Quality of Service (QoS) configuration to prioritize critical applications
- Network equipment and cabling maintenance

### Why choose us

We understand that internet downtime means business downtime. That is why we design high-availability solutions backed by a 24/7 Network Operations Center (NOC), so problems are detected and resolved before they impact your operations.
