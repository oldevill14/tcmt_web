## End-to-end Data Center Solutions

TCM Technology Co., Ltd. provides complete data center design and build services — from requirements assessment and infrastructure design through to the installation of servers, storage systems, internal data center networking, power backup and precision cooling. We ensure your data center operates continuously, securely and at peak efficiency.

### Scope of work

- Data center design to TIA-942 / Uptime Institute standards
- Installation of enterprise-grade servers and storage systems
- Internal data center networking (Top-of-Rack / Spine-Leaf architectures)
- Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) and backup generator systems
- Precision air conditioning systems
- DCIM and environmental monitoring
- 24/7 maintenance and technical support

### Why choose us

We partner with leading manufacturers including Dell, Fujitsu and Cisco. Our certified engineers design and manage systems to meet your organization's current and future needs, with a commitment to high availability and business continuity.
