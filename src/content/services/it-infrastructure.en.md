## End-to-end IT Infrastructure Services

TCM Technology Co., Ltd. designs and installs complete IT infrastructure — LAN (Local Area Network) and server environments — that are stable, secure and ready to grow with your organization. We use equipment from world-leading manufacturers to deliver solutions that stand the test of time.

### Scope of work

- Survey, design and documentation of IT infrastructure architecture
- Structured cabling installation (CAT6 / CAT6A / Fiber Optic)
- On-premises and Hybrid Cloud server installation and configuration
- Virtualization (VMware, Hyper-V) for efficient resource utilization
- NAS / SAN storage systems with integrated backup solutions
- Directory Services and Identity Management
- Ongoing infrastructure maintenance and upgrades

With over 25 years of experience delivering IT infrastructure projects for leading government agencies and private enterprises, our certified engineers consistently deliver quality solutions on time and within budget.
