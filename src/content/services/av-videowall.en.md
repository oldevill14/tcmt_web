## Video Wall & AV Display Solutions

TCM Technology Co., Ltd. designs and installs large-format display systems (Video Wall) and AV (Audio-Visual) systems for control rooms, boardrooms, corporate lobbies and exhibition spaces. We combine cutting-edge display technology with professional-grade management software for reliable, visually impressive results.

### Scope of work

- Design and installation of Video Wall systems (LCD / LED / DLP) of any size
- Matrix switching and IP-based AV distribution systems
- Content Management Software for display control and scheduling
- Interactive display and touch screen systems
- Digital Signage for internal corporate communications
- Audio systems for meeting rooms and public areas
- Post-installation maintenance and technical support

### Why choose us

We partner with leading display manufacturers including Epson and ASUS. Our AV and video wall specialists design solutions precisely matched to your use case and budget, delivering installations that make an impact from day one.
