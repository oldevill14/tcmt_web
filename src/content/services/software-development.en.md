## End-to-end Software & Web Development

TCM Technology Co., Ltd. designs and builds custom software and web applications tailored to the specific needs of your organization. With a team experienced in both IT infrastructure and software development, we deliver true end-to-end solutions — bridging hardware and software seamlessly under one roof.

### Scope of work

- **Corporate Websites** — Design and develop professional websites for government agencies and private enterprises. Fully responsive, WCAG-accessible, and PDPA-compliant.
- **Web Applications** — Build online systems that map directly to your operational workflows: registration portals, reporting dashboards, service portals and more.
- **Custom Software** — Analyse, design and build bespoke software systems for specialized business functions, from procurement to workflow automation.
- **System Integration** — Connect disparate software platforms via API and middleware, including tight integration with the IT infrastructure we deploy.
- **Mobile Applications** — Develop iOS and Android applications connected to your organization's backend systems.
- **Maintenance & Enhancement** — Provide ongoing software support after delivery: feature updates, performance tuning and issue resolution.

### Our edge

As a System Integrator with expertise across both hardware and software, TCM Technology designs solutions where the software layer connects seamlessly with the physical IT infrastructure. This eliminates the coordination overhead of managing multiple vendors, and ensures every component performs as a coherent whole.
