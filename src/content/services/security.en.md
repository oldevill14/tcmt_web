## Comprehensive IT Security Solutions

As cyber threats grow increasingly sophisticated, TCM Technology Co., Ltd. provides full-spectrum information security services — from assessment and design through to the installation of security systems at every layer, from the network to the application. We safeguard your organization's data assets and systems with proven, enterprise-grade solutions.

### Scope of work

- Risk assessment and vulnerability analysis
- Firewall and Next-Generation Firewall (NGFW) installation and configuration
- Intrusion Detection and Prevention Systems (IDS/IPS)
- Anti-malware and Endpoint Protection platforms
- VPN systems for secure remote access
- Security Information and Event Management (SIEM)
- Security Awareness Training for staff
- Ongoing security system maintenance and monitoring

### Why choose us

Our certified security engineers bring experience protecting large government agencies and private enterprises. We provide 24-hour Incident Response services, ensuring your organization can recover quickly when it matters most.
