## HVAC Systems for Server Rooms and Organizations

TCM Technology Co., Ltd. designs, installs and maintains HVAC (Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning) systems with a specialist focus on server rooms and data centers, where precision temperature and humidity control is critical. We also provide general air conditioning solutions for offices and commercial buildings.

### Scope of work

- HVAC system design to server room and data center standards
- Precision Air Conditioning (PAC) installation for accurate temperature and humidity control
- VRF / VRV air conditioning systems for office buildings
- Ventilation and air filtration systems
- Real-time temperature and humidity monitoring
- Scheduled preventive maintenance
- Emergency repair and troubleshooting

### Why choose us

Our specialist technicians bring hands-on experience installing precision HVAC systems in high-stakes server room environments. We work alongside your IT team to ensure the cooling infrastructure is correctly sized for your equipment load — protecting your investment and eliminating thermal risk.
