## End-to-end Networking Services

TCM Technology Co., Ltd. designs, installs and maintains enterprise networks — from
network architecture and the deployment of switches, routers and firewalls, through to
security policy and monitoring. We keep your systems running continuously and securely,
to international standards.

### Scope of work

- Requirements assessment and network architecture design
- Installation and configuration of enterprise-grade network equipment
- Wireless (Wi-Fi) network deployment with full coverage
- Network management and monitoring systems
- Post-deployment maintenance and technical support

### Why choose us

With over 25 years of experience and engineers certified by leading manufacturers, we
deliver networking solutions tailored to your organization — across both the public and
private sectors.
